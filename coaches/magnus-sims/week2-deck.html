🔑 Critical Teaching Points - Poolside Reference
PRESS WHEN:
• Protecting late lead
• Opponent weak ballhandlers
• Need urgent turnover
• Player advantage (exclusion)
ZONE WHEN:
• Opponent skilled drivers
• Foul trouble management
• Conserving energy
• Even/disadvantage situations
COUNTER 3-SEC RULE:
• Outlet to ready player
• Scan for outnumber (3v2, 4v3)
• Attack if advantage exists
• Pull back if even/disadvantage
DRIVE-REPLACE KEYS:
• 4-5m spacing (touchable = too close)
• See gap BEFORE driving
• Non-drivers replace to open spots
• Motion continues when ball inside
GK COMMUNICATION:
• Organize before ball moves
• Key phrases: "Step!", "Ball pressure!", "Help side!"
• Vocal = leadership development
• Praise attempts, refine accuracy
LATE-GAME MINDSET:
• Clock awareness constant
• Shot selection = championship shots only
• Foul management critical
• Composure > urgency

💭 COACH'S QUICK REFERENCE - Critical Reminders
Your Team Profile: 4 solid seniors (no game changers), 3 juniors (1 GK + 2 field developing), multiple underclassmen with high IQ but developing physically. Mixed stage = differentiate teaching.
  • Seniors: Leadership opportunities (Friday warm-up, peer coaching Thursday). "Solid" = maximize through responsibility.
  • Juniors: GK needs confidence-building through communication drills. Two field players "took big steps"—capitalize momentum with advanced reps.
  • Underclassmen: High IQ + developing physically = praise decisions, be patient with execution. Pattern recognition now, power later.
When to Intervene (Scrimmages):
  • ✓ Teachable moment (perfect execution—freeze, highlight, resume)
  • ✓ Repeated mistake (3+ occurrences—stop, fix, immediate do-over)
  • ✓ Decision-point failure (team "guesses" instead of reads situation)
  • ✗ Single execution errors (let them learn from consequences)
Energy Management: Monday/Wednesday moderate intensity (learning focus). Tuesday/Thursday high intensity (application/testing). Friday moderate-high (synthesis). 5+ sessions/week = manage fatigue carefully. Week 1 → Easterns Path: Foundation (Wk 1-4) → Refinement (Wk 5-8) → Competition (Wk 9-12) → Peak (Wk 13+). This week = establishing baseline. Perfection not expected; progress required.
