Speed Without Size — Maximize What You Have
Fast, skilled, small players with an elite goalie. This week builds a transition-first identity — win with counter-attack speed before opponents can set, and run a press that denies entries rather than fighting size-on-size. By Friday: 70%+ counter-attack conversion, press denies center passes, GK outlets within 2 sec.

Drill Day Key Cue
Counter Attack Lanes Mon Read open lane before you pass; outlet receiver catches and attacks in one motion
Advantage Rules (3v2, 4v3) Mon, Thu Don't slow down — if you have numbers, attack the goal
Pressure Passing Tue Stick in the lane, body position denies — don't reach
Center D Ball-Side Positioning Tue Stay ball-side — if ball is on the right, your body is on the right side of center-forward
3-3 Primary Look (6x5) Wed If center-forward open, pass. If wing pocket open, skip. If nothing, reset to point
GK Outlet Mon Save, scan, outlet — no pause. Your outlet starts the counter attack
Tracking Priorities
  • Counter conversion: Conversions / numerical advantages — target 70%+
  • Press denial: Forced perimeter passes / center entries attempted
  • GK outlet speed: Save to outlet — target ≤ 2 sec
  • 6x5 first look: Shots from first read / total shots — target 60%+
Coach Decisions
  • Mon: Name top 3 counter-attack outlet receivers
  • Tue: Name center defender starter by number
  • Wed: Lock primary 6x5 unit (6 players)
  • Thu: Name transition starting five
Watch For
  • Small players fighting size-on-size at center D — cue "position beats size"
  • Counter turning into footrace with no spacing — cue "lanes, not chaos"
  • GK hesitating on outlet — cue "outlet within 2 sec or call 'hold'"
  • Press defenders ball-watching — cue "your job is the pass, not the ball"
