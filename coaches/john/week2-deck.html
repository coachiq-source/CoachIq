Week 2 Focus
When the first play breaks down, players freeze — no backup plan
Outcome: Every player can name the backup decision ("If my pass is covered, I turn and look for open space"). At least 8 players demonstrate it in live play without a coach prompt.

May 3

Fun and learning

Conditioning
Tue: Dynamic warm-up (5 min) + partner passing while jogging. Thu: Dynamic warm-up (5 min) + partner chase tag (3 min).
Goalie Track
Rotate 2–3 players through the crease. Focus: stick positioning (stick up), stance (knees bent), tracking the ball. 10 soft shots from 8m each session. Goalies join all scrimmage blocks.

Focal Drills — Quick Reference
Drill Day Key Cue
Partner Passing (Box Position) Tue "Box position — top hand at armpit, stick vertical"
Noodle Scooping Tue "Butt down, top hand down, scoop through it"
3v2 Handball (Backup Decision) Tue "If your pass is covered, turn and find open space"
Form Cradling + Zig Zag Cradling Thu "Rock the stick, don't squeeze it"
1v1 Ground Balls Thu "Box out with your hips, scoop away from pressure"
4v3 Fast Clear Thu "Push it up — don't wait for them to set"
Tracking Priorities
  • Backup Decision: Count players who turn and find space when first pass is covered (target: 6+)
  • Ground Ball Wins: Track top 3 by name
  • Catch Success: 10 in a row in box position (target: 10+ players)
Coach Decisions
  • Tue: Identify top 3 ground-ball players by name — they start every GB drill Thu
  • Thu: Name starting goalie for first game — they get every scrimmage rep in Week 3
Watch For
  • Players bend at waist on GBs — cue "Butt down"
  • Top hand choked up on plastic — cue "Hand down the shaft"
  • Players squeeze the stick — cue "Hold it, don't squeeze it"
  • Shooting space violations (girls' rule) — stop play, explain, reset
