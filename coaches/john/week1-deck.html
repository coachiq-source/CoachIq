Week 1 Field Sheet
Ground balls, stick skills, and learning what the field looks like.
Outcome: Every player can cradle while jogging, scoop a rolling ball without stopping their feet, and complete a catch-and-throw sequence with a partner from 5 yards.

May 1

Turf / Grass (TBD)
Conditioning — Movement Foundation
No dedicated conditioning. All movement inside drills. Each session: 5-min dynamic warm-up (high knees, butt kicks, side shuffles, carioca — half speed). Water every 10 min.
Goalie Track — Position Unsettled
S1: No goalie; coach demos crease positioning. S2: Invite volunteers to try goalie for one 10-min block. Exposure, not evaluation.
Session 1
Stick Skills
0–5 Warm-Up · High knees, butt kicks, shuffles, 2 laps
5–15 Hand Cradling · Teach stick parts, cradle motion (no ball)
15–25 Coach Toss + Partner Passing · First catches (3 yds)
25–35 Noodle Scooping · Ground ball intro (no defense)
35–43 3v3 Handball · Small-sided, no sticks
43–45 Cool-Down · Walk 2 laps, message + callout
Session 2
GB + Clearing
0–5 Warm-Up · Add backward jog; cradle while jogging
5–12 Form Cradle + Passing · Review (7 yds, 5/10 target)
12–22 1v1 GB's · Ground ball battles (hips only)
22–32 4v3 Box · Clearing intro (no ride, cross midfield)
32–42 3v3 Short Field · Constraint: 2 passes before shot
42–45 Cool-Down · Walk 2 laps, message
Drill Session Key Cue
Hand Cradling S1 Top hand stays on the shaft — don't slide it down
Coach Toss S1 See the ball into the pocket
Partner Passing Both Catch two hands, look, then pass
Noodle Scooping S1 Butt down, top hand down, scoop through it
1v1 GB's S2 Box out with your hips, scoop away from pressure
4v3 Box S2 Goalie looks wide first; defenders spread out
Tracking Priorities
  • GB Attempts — who goes for the ball vs. who watches
  • Catch Rate — 6/10 target by week end
  • Cradle Hand Position — top hand on shaft, not plastic
  • Whistle-Stop — 2-sec reaction
Coach Decisions
  • S1: Top 3 GB players by name (demo S2)
  • S2: Name starting goalie or decide rotation for first game
Watch For
  • Waist bend (not hip drop) on GB's
  • Top hand slides to plastic
  • Throw before look
  • Feet stop to scoop
  • Ball crowding in 3v3
  • Frustration when drops happen
