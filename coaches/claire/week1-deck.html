Claire
Girls U16 Lacrosse
Week 1 Focus
Players uncertain on basic positioning and ground-ball approach. Zone defense looks solid on paper but breaks when ball movement speeds up.
End of Week: Every player can state "ball, help left, help right" and demonstrate it in a live 3v3. Ground balls are scooped cleanly 7 out of 10 reps.
First Game
April 4
Primary Target
Fun and learning
Field
Standard field
Conditioning
Session 1: 2 × 20-yard shuttle runs (light jog recovery).
Session 2: 3 × 30-yard shuttle runs. All conditioning stays under 3 min total.
Goalie Track
Position unsettled. Coach feeds to empty net or placeholder goalie for live pressure. Once decided: 200 soft catches + 3 rounds low/hip/high feeds. Communication: call "clear" on outlets.
Session 1
GB + Zone Intro
0–8: Dynamic warm-up + partner passing (10 yards).
8–18: Noodle Scooping — "Butt down, top hand down, scoop through it." 8 reps.
18–35: 1v1 GB's — box out, scoop, outlet. 6 reps.
35–50: Shell Drill — 2-2-2 zone shape, walk-through. Call "ball, help left, help right."
50–60: 3v3 half-field. Constraint: 3 passes before shooting.
Session 2
Clearing + Set Offense
0–8: Dynamic warm-up + partner passing (12 yards). Add Bad Pass Drill.
8–18: 4v3 Box Clear — goalie → wing → midfield → cross midline. 8 reps.
18–35: 4 Corners Pass + Star Drill. Goal: 10 consecutive completions.
35–50: 2-Man Game (Pick & Roll) — 2v2 live, 5 min.
50–60: 6v6 scrimmage. Constraint: Every possession includes one clear and one pick.
Drill Day Key Cue
Noodle Scooping Session 1 "Butt down, top hand down, scoop through it."
1v1 GB's Session 1 "Box out with your hips, then scoop away from pressure."
Shell Drill (2-2-2) Session 1 "Ball, help left, help right."
4v3 Box Clear Session 2 "Goalie looks wing first; defenders spread wide."
4 Corners Pass Session 2 "Catch two hands, look at the goal, then pass."
2-Man Game (Pick & Roll) Session 2 "Set the pick with feet set, roll to the ball."
Tracking Priorities
  • Ground ball win rate (60%+ target)
  • Zone shape breaks (≤5 per session)
  • Passing completion (75%+ target)
  • Ball-Help-Help verbal cues (10+ players)
Coach Decisions
  • Session 1: Identify top 3 GB players by name
  • Session 2: Name first-line clearing unit (4 players)
Watch For
  • Players bending at waist on GBs — cue "butt down"
  • Top hand choked up on plastic — cue "hand down shaft"
  • Zone defenders chasing ball instead of holding area
  • Players not calling "ball, help left, help right"
