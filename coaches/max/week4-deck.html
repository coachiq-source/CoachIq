Week 4 Focus
Getting the ball up the field without turnovers — clearing with patience and purpose
End of Week: Goalie calls "clear" or "push" after every save. Wings know which lane to fill. Team crosses midfield with possession 7 out of 10 attempts.

Tracking Priorities
  • Clear Success Rate: Clears that reach offensive box / attempted clears. Target: 7/10 by Friday.
  • Ground Ball Win Rate: Scooped cleanly / contested GBs. Target: 50%+.
  • Goalie Outlet Accuracy: Successful outlets / total saves. Target: 6/10.
  • Transition Shot Rate: Shots in first 10 sec after turnover. Target: 4+ per scrimmage.
Coach Decisions
  • By Tue end: Identify top 3 ground-ball players by name.
  • By Thu end: Pick first-line clearing unit (goalie + 3 defenders) by jersey number.
  • Before next week: Lock the goalie's verbal call protocol — practice it in warm-up every session.
Watch For
  • Goalie hesitates on the call — must call within 2 sec of the save.
  • Wings don't spread on "clear" — they stay bunched near midfield.
  • Midfielder carries too long — gets stripped at midfield. Cue: "Two touches."
  • Pick-and-roll screener moves early — feet must be set.
  • Players call for ball but don't move to open space.
