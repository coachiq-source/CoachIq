Noodle Scooping Mon Butt down, top hand down, scoop through it

Watch For
  • Bending at waist on GBs → cue "butt down"
  • Top hand on plastic → cue "hand down the shaft"
  • One-hand catches → reset to two hands
  • Passes too hard → cue "soft hands"
  • No fish-hook after scooping
  • Goalies flat-footed
