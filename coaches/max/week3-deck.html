15–27: Partner Passing + Shooting Lines

Focal Drill Quick Reference
Drill Session Key Cue
Noodle Scooping 1 "Butt down, top hand down, scoop through it."
1v1 GB's 1, 2 "Box out with your hips, then scoop away from pressure."
2v1 GB's 2 "Scoop, turn, find the outlet in one motion."
4v3 Box Clear 2 "Goalie looks wing first; defenders spread wide."
Scoop and Shoot 3 "Scoop through it, look at the goal, then shoot."
Partner Passing 1, 3 "Catch two hands, look at your partner, then pass."
Tracking Priorities
  • GB win rate: 60%+ target
  • Clear completion: 50%+ to O box
  • Shots on cage: 40%+ target
  • Goalie outlet: ≤ 3 sec
Coach Decisions
  • S1: Name top 3 GB players
  • S2: Pick first-line clearing unit (G + 3D)
  • S3: Name starting transition unit (top 4)
Watch For
  • Choking up on stick — cue "hand down"
  • Bending at waist — cue "butt down"
  • Goalie clears late — teach "look wing first"
  • Players bunching up — spread them wide
