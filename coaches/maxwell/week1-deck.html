Ground balls and clearing — same problem: we don't know where to move after we pick up the ball.
End of Week 1: Players scoop a ground ball and immediately turn to find a teammate. On clears, the goalie looks to a wing and the wing looks up-field. No more scooping and freezing.

Conditioning
U10 age-appropriate: dynamic warm-up (5 min — high knees, butt kicks, shuffles, carioca, skips) + partner passing (3 min — catch with two hands, box position). Total: 8 min. No fatigue before teaching.
Goalie Track
Position unsettled. Every session: 5-min intro block (rotate interested players). Session 1: crease positioning. Session 2: soft hands (tennis balls). Session 3: basic saves (coach rolls soft shots from 8 feet). Goal: identify one player by Friday.
Session 1
Fundamentals
0–8 Warm-Up + Passing
Dynamic movements (5 min) + partner passing (3 min)

Entry-level ground balls: butt down, top hand down, scoop through

5 ft → 10 ft → add one cradle before passing
28–38 4v3 Box (Clearing Intro)
Goalie to wing, walk-through (no defense), 8 reps

Crease positioning, water, one teach point
Session 2
Transition
0–8 Warm-Up + Passing
Same as Session 1
8–18 Scoop and Shoot
Scoop-pivot-look-shoot in one motion, 8 reps per player
18–30 2v1 GB's
Scoop and outlet to open teammate, light pressure, 10 reps
30–38 4v3 Box with Defense
Goalie outlets to wing, add 2 light defenders, 6 reps
38–45 Goalie Intro + Cool-Down
Soft hands (tennis balls), water, one teach point
Session 3
Scrimmage
0–8 Light Activation
Dynamic warm-up (3 min) + partner passing (5 min, to 15 ft)
8–18 GB Speed Round
1v1 GB's, winner stays, loser rotates, 15 total reps
18–38 3v3 Scrimmage
Constraint: every possession starts with a ground ball. One pass before shooting.
38–45 Goalie Saves + Cool-Down
Basic saves (coach rolls shots), partner passing, huddle
Success = 8/12 scoop-and-turn · 3 goalie outlets · 5+ name callouts
Drill Session Key Cue
Noodle Scooping Session 1 "Butt down, top hand down, scoop through it."
Scoop and Shoot Session 2 "Scoop, turn, look, shoot — one motion, no pause."
2v1 GB's Session 2 "Whoever scoops, turn and find your partner."
4v3 Box Session 1, 2 "Goalie calls the name, wing catches and looks up-field."
1v1 GB's Session 3 "Scoop it, turn away, outlet to me."
Tracking Priorities
  • Scoop-turn-look rate (no pause after scooping)
  • Clear completion (goalie to wing, target 5/10 by Fri)
  • Catch success rate (partner passing)
  • Cradle comfort while jogging (8/12 by Fri)
  • Outlet identification (name callouts)
Coach Decisions
  • Session 1: ID top 3 GB players by name
  • Session 2: Pick one goalie candidate for Friday scrimmage
  • Session 3: Decide if goalie candidate keeps trying
Watch For
  • Bend at waist, not hips (cue "butt down")
  • Scoop and stand still (demo fish-hook pivot)
  • Goalie holds ball too long (cue "call the name first")
  • Top hand choked up (cue "hand down the shaft")
  • One-handed catching (cue "two hands, box position")
  • Drop ball when running (normal — add form cradling Week 2)
