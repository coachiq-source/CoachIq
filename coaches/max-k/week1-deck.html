Drill Day Key Cue
Counter Attack Lanes Mon Sprint-swim to your lane before the GK turns — outlet is a race.
Advantage Rules (3v2 / 4v3) Mon, Thu Wings wide, center reads — make the defense choose.
Center Defender Ball-Side Tue Ball / Player / Area — say it before every rep. Deny the entry.
Drive and Defend (M-Zone) Tue Stay in your gap — don't chase to the perimeter. Box entry is the only failure.
3-3 Primary Look Wed Read the defender's hips — if they turn to ball, skip it. If they sag to post, direct entry.
2v2 Canada GB → Counter Thu Scoop through it, turn, find the outlet — one motion.
Tracking Priorities
  • Primary Counter Conversion: 1v0 & 2v1 attempts vs. shots (≥70%)
  • GK Outlet Speed: Save to outlet in ≤2 sec
  • Wing Separation: Clean outlets without defender contact
  • Center-Forward Entry: Possessions reaching post (≥60%)
Coach Decisions
  • Mon: ID top 3 wings by name
  • Tue: Select starting center defender
  • Wed: Lock primary 6x5 unit (6 players)
  • Thu: Name first-line counter unit (4 players + GK)
Watch For
  • Wings breaking too early → defender intercepts outlet
  • GK holding ball too long (≥3 sec) → counter window closes
  • Center defender chasing perimeter → box-entry lane opens
  • Wings drifting to center → eliminates numbers-up advantage
