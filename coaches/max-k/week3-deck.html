Drill Day Key Cue
Noodle Scooping Mon "Butt down, top hand down, scoop through it."
1v1 GB's Mon "Box out with your hips, then scoop away from pressure."
3v2 West Genny Tue "Spread wide, pass to the open man, attack the cage when you have space."
4v3 Box Mon "Goalie looks wing first; defenders spread wide."
Fast Breaks Tue "Ball-carrier pushes, wings spread, trailer fills the middle."
2-Man Game (Pick & Roll) Wed "Set the pick with feet set, roll to the ball."
Tracking Priorities
  • GB Win Rate: Clean scoops / contested GBs (target: >60%)
  • Transition Shot Rate: Shots in first 10 sec (target: >40%)
  • Outlet Pass Completion: GB-to-outlet success (target: >50%)
  • 3v2 Finish Rate: Goals or quality shots (target: >60%)
Coach Decisions
  • Mon: Identify top 3 GB players by name
  • Tue: Pick first-line transition unit (top 4)
  • Wed: Name starting attack line (3 players)
  • Thu: Match fastest defenders to opponent threats
  • Fri: Review KPI log — adjust Week 4 if needed
Watch For
  • Players bending at the waist on GBs instead of dropping hips
  • Ball-carrier looks down after scoop (kills outlet vision)
  • On 3v2, players bunch instead of spreading wide
  • Goalie clears too late (outlets should be available at save)
  • Defenders stop short on help — gives free shooting lane
  • Top hand choked up on plastic instead of down the shaft
