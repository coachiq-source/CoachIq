Focal Drill Quick-Reference
Counter Attack Lanes
Mon / Wed
GK calls outlet direction. Sprint-swim to lane before pass. Never walk — recovery swim back.
Advantage Rules
Mon / Wed / Fri
Attack immediately. No holding. If you see goal, shoot. Defense — sprint-swim to recover.
3-3 Primary Look
Tue / Thu
Read defender's hip. If drive is closed, cross to pocket or replace. Never force first look.
"Primary Closed"
Thu
When I call "primary closed," first option is dead. Find second look in 3 seconds or reset.
Live 6x5
Thu / Fri
First look 1-in/2-down. If not there in 10s, read 5-in/4-down. Shot clock is deadline.
5x6 Post Protection
Tue
GK owns shape. When you hear "set," check position. Protect posts. Make them shoot outside.
